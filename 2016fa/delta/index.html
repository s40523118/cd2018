DeltaBot Calculator

This is a prototype of a DeltaBot Calculator which works like the well-known spreadsheet while providing helpful graphical feedback, and (soon) sliders and controls to customize the geometry. The calculation is a little off, as I haven't accounted for carriage size.

  • ARROWS - Move the effector in the XY plane
  • SHIFT + UP/DOWN - Move in the Z plane
  • SPACE - Home the effector
  • J L - Change the bot radius
  • I K - Change the bot height
  • G H - Change the rod spacing
  • Z - Randomize the bot size
